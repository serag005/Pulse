Help Center

Getting Started

Creating an Account

Click on "Log In" in the top right corner of our website and select "Create Account." Fill in your information and follow the verification steps.

Browsing Products

Navigate through our categories or use the search bar to find specific products. Filter results by category, price, or other attributes.

Ordering Process

Adding Items to Cart

Click the "Add to Cart" button on any product page. You can review your cart by clicking the cart icon in the top right corner.

Checkout Process

When you're ready to complete your purchase, click "Checkout" in your cart. Follow the steps to enter shipping and payment information.

Order Tracking

After placing an order, you'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking information. You can also check order status in your account dashboard.

Product Information

Custom Prosthetics

For customized prosthetic devices, additional measurements or consultation may be required. Instructions will be provided during the ordering process.

Product Care

Each product comes with specific care instructions. Generally, keep prosthetics clean and dry, and follow maintenance schedules.
