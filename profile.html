Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in, provide shipping and payment information, and confirm your order.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting our customer service. After this period, orders are processed and cannot be modified or canceled.

Are your prosthetic products customizable?

Yes, many of our prosthetic products can be customized to fit your specific needs. Look for the "Customizable" label on product pages. Additional information and measurements may be required for customization.

What is the difference between upper and lower limb prosthetics?

Upper limb prosthetics are designed for arms and hands, while lower limb prosthetics are for legs and feet. Each type is engineered with specific functionalities to support different activities and mobility needs.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days. Custom products may take longer, with estimated delivery times provided during checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location, typically ranging from 7-21 business days. Additional customs fees may apply depending on your country.

What happens if I'm not home when my order arrives?

For most shipments, the carrier will leave your package at your door if no signature is required. For orders requiring a signature, they will leave a delivery attempt notice with instructions for rescheduling or pickup.

Can I change my delivery address after placing my order?

Address changes may be possible if your order hasn't shipped yet. Please contact customer service immediately with your order number to request an address change.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Standard and expedited shipping typically only deliver on business days (Monday-Friday). Select carriers may offer weekend delivery for Next Day service in certain areas.

What is your return policy?

Standard products can be returned within 30 days of delivery if they're in original condition. Custom products are non-returnable unless defective. Contact customer service to initiate a return.

How do I return a product?

To return a product, log into your account, go to order history, select the item you wish to return, and follow the return instructions. You'll receive a return shipping label and further instructions via email.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the item price.

Return Eligibility:

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Special order items and clearance sales may have different policies

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Custom or personalized products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Products with broken seals or missing components

Exchange Process

Exchanges can be processed for the same item in a different size or color, subject to availability.

How to Request an Exchange:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Navigate to your order history
  3. Select the order containing the item(s) for exchange
  4. Follow the exchange instructions

Please note that exchanges are subject to inventory availability. If your desired item is unavailable, we'll process a return instead.

How to Start a Return

To return an item, please follow these instructions:

  1. Locate your order number from your confirmation email or account history
  2. Contact our customer service team at returns@example.com with your order number in the subject line
  3. Include the reason for your return in the email body
  4. Our team will respond within 24 hours with a return authorization and shipping instructions
  5. Package your item(s) in their original packaging with all included components
  6. Attach the provided return label to your package
  7. Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location

Return processing typically takes 7-10 business days from receipt of your returned items.
